Steve Jobs

The guy from Apple

Jobs in the Worldwide Developer Conference 2010 showing the iPhone 4

Timeline

  • 1995 - Born in San Francisco, later adopted by Paul Jobs and Clara Hagopian

  • 1961 - Moves to Palo Alto, started assiting Cupertino Middle School

  • 1972 - Enters Reed Collegue University in Portland Oregon

  • 1974 - Returns to California and starts working for Atari Inc.

  • 1976 - Founds Apple Computer Company with Steve Wozniak

  • 1983 - Creation of Apple Lisa, the first computer of the company with graphic user interface

  • 1984 - Creation of Apple Macintosh, the first computer of the company with a mouse.

  • 1985 - Steve leaves Apple and founds NeXT Computer

  • 1986 - Steve buys The Graphics Group, later Pixar

  • 1988 - The first NeXT computer is released

  • 1993 - Jobs decides to focus the company only on software, changing the name to Next Software Inc.

  • 1995 - Toy Story, the first completly aminated movie, is released.

  • 1996 - Apple buys NeXT Software and consequently Steve Jobs returns to Apple.

  • 2001 - Steve presents the iPod

  • 2006 - Disney buys Pixar, Steve Jobs becomes the largest shareholder of Disney

  • 2011 - Dies at age 56
